Our Work

“Electricians are responsible for installing new electrical circuits, testing, fitting, repairing and rewiring existing circuits. It is generally considered a demanding career and comes with certain dangers and risks.”

An electrician is responsible for all kinds of electrical work like fitting, rewiring, repairing, testing, installing and maintaining electrical systems and components.

Some job responsibilities of an electrician may include:

  • installing and repairing circuits, systems and wiring
  • connecting electrical systems to power lines that provide electricity to establishments
  • testing electrical systems to ensure proper installation
  • executing electric wiring plans according to electrical blueprints
  • installing safety and distribution components
  • conducting preventive maintenance to prevent the breakdown of old wiring and systems
  • 24/7 SUPPORT
  • troubleshooting to identify the root cause of malfunctions and correcting the damaged units or faulty wiring
